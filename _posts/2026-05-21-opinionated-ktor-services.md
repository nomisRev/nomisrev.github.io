Today I gave a talk at KotlinConf 2026 in Munich about "Opinionated Ktor Services". 
In this talk, I shared my experience and opinions on how to build robust, maintainable, and scalable services using Ktor and the Kotlin ecosystem.

Building services with Ktor is a lot of fun, but because it is so flexible, it can sometimes be hard to decide on a structure or which libraries to use.
This talk aims to provide a set of "opinions" that have worked well for me and the teams I've worked with.
